As president of Rehabilicare Trust,I am proud to have this opportunity to share my thoughts and ideas. The Rehabilicare is now entering its 5th year in serving the residents of Rawalpindi islamabad and also from the whole pakistan in which included the remote areas of anterior punjab ,Sind and even from gilgit particularly those who are considered underserved and neglected.
Man is born to serve his fellow man. RehabiliCare Trust is working for the uplift of humanity. It has been observed that 4 out of 10 persons are suffering with some disability. RehabilicareTrust is one of its kind aiming to provide quality treatment at a minimal cost. It is indeed a matter of great pleasure and honor for me to be the president of an organization like RehabiliCare Trust, where the patients are treated with utmost care and attention and best possible treatment is provided to them despite of cast, colour and creed.

Establishment of the Health care center, education, food, shelter, special education school for special people. State of art Rehabilitation Center for physically and mentally impaired people, without the discrimination as to Sex,Color, Religion ,Sect ,Caste, or Country and free from all sort of Political Motives.

 

Life is a gift from the creator, sometimes Almighty Allah for the sake of our understanding and creating a sense of thankfulness we see people around us who are not gifted with all those things which are imperative to lead a normal life. Such happenings are either incidental or by birth. In such situations the choice is our own either let these special people become a burden for their own self and for those around them or help them lead a life close to normal.
